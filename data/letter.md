# Letter Found at the Entrance

To whoever enters after me,

If this letter has found you, then the library is already gone.

Do not trust the silence.

Most visitors believe the books disappeared first.

They are wrong.

The books remained long after the library itself vanished.

I know how absurd that sounds.

For now, there are only three things you should know:

1. The final librarian was the last person seen inside.
2. The eastern wing was sealed three days before the disappearance.
3. Something beneath the library was never meant to be opened.

I wish I could tell you more.

Unfortunately, certain memories refuse to reveal themselves until the correct question is asked.

Until then, this page will remain incomplete.

— A Friend of the Library
