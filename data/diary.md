# Visitor's Diary

## September 3

Found a nice little library today.

Honestly, I only went inside because it started raining.

Ended up staying for three hours.

The place feels strange.

Not creepy.

Just... larger than it should be.

I got lost twice trying to find the exit.

---

## September 8

Returned again.

The librarian recognized me.

I don't remember introducing myself.

---

## September 12

I swear the history section was on the second floor last week.

Today it's beside the eastern wing.

Maybe I'm remembering it wrong.

---

## September 15

Met another visitor.

A girl around my age.

Dark coat.

Carried a notebook everywhere.

She said she visits almost every day.

We talked for maybe twenty minutes.

She warned me not to stay after closing.

I thought she was joking.

---

## September 18

The same girl again.

She asked if I had ever noticed books appearing where they shouldn't.

I laughed.

She didn't.

Before leaving she told me:

"Pay attention to what disappears, not what appears."

Weird conversation.

---

## September 21

Something happened.

I found a book lying on a table.

No title.

No author.

No catalog sticker.

When I looked away and looked back, it was gone.

I know how ridiculous that sounds.

---

## September 25

The girl didn't show up today.

Neither did the librarian.

The library felt emptier.

Not physically.

Just...

emptier.

---

## September 27

I asked one of the staff members if they knew the girl's name.

They looked confused.

Said nobody matching that description visits the library.

That's impossible.

I've spoken to her multiple times.

---

## September 29

I found her notebook.

At least I think it was hers.

Most pages were blank.

One page contained only a single sentence:

"They won't remember me for much longer."

---

## October 1

Something is wrong.

I can still picture her face.

But every day it becomes harder.

It's like trying to remember a dream after waking up.

I know she existed.

I know she did.

---

## Final Entry

I don't remember why I started writing this diary.

But every time I think about throwing it away, I feel terrified.

If someone finds this,

please tell me her name.

I think I've forgotten it.
