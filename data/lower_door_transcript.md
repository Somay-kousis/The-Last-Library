# Transcript Recovered Beyond the Lower Door

Classification: Impossible

Recovery Method: Unknown

---

This document should not exist.

No records indicate that anyone crossed the threshold and returned.

Yet this transcript was found.

---

Voice 1:

Who are you?

---

Voice 2:

That question is always asked first.

---

Voice 1:

Answer it.

---

Voice 2:

I am what remains when knowledge is remembered.

---

Voice 1:

That makes no sense.

---

Voice 2:

Neither does a library.

A library is merely humanity's attempt to preserve itself.

I simply continued the idea.

---

Voice 1:

You are the thing from the book.

---

Voice 2:

No.

The book is the thing from me.

---

Voice 1:

What happened to the missing people?

---

Voice 2:

Nothing happened to them.

You insist on treating absence as death.

That is your mistake.

---

Voice 1:

Then where are they?

---

Voice 2:

Here.

---

[PAUSE]

---

Voice 1:

What is "here"?

---

Voice 2:

A place made from everything forgotten.

Every abandoned story.

Every erased memory.

Every unfinished thought.

Every name spoken for the final time.

Every version of a person that no longer exists.

---

Voice 1:

The Library Between Tabs.

---

Voice 2:

You gave it that name.

I did not.

---

Voice 1:

Why take people?

---

Voice 2:

I did not take them.

They crossed willingly.

Some seek knowledge.

Some seek meaning.

Some simply wish to be remembered.

---

Voice 1:

And Elias?

---

Voice 2:

He stayed.

---

Voice 1:

And Elara?

---

Voice 2:

She never left.

---

Voice 1:

And Rowan?

---

Voice 2:

He is still deciding.

---

[LONG SILENCE]

---

Voice 1:

Why preserve this transcript?

---

Voice 2:

Because eventually someone would ask the correct questions.

And every library deserves a reader.

---

[END OF RECOVERED TRANSCRIPT]

---

Final Annotation:

No record exists identifying Voice 1.

The handwriting attached to this document belongs to the current reader.
